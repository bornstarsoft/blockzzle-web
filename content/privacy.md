# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2025-12-22

Blockzzle (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect and how we use it when you visit **blockzzle.com**.

## 1) What we collect

### A. Analytics data (optional)
If analytics is enabled, we may collect basic usage data (such as pages visited, device/browser info, and referral source) to understand traffic and improve the site.

### B. Cookies and similar technologies
Analytics tools may use cookies or similar technologies to measure usage and improve the website.

### C. Contact messages
If you email us, we will receive the information you include (such as your email address and the contents of your message).

## 2) How we use information
We use the information to:
- operate, maintain, and improve Blockzzle
- understand which pages and game links are helpful
- detect abuse, prevent fraud, and maintain security
- respond to inquiries and support requests

## 3) Sharing
We do **not** sell your personal information.

We may share limited data with service providers (for example, analytics vendors such as Google) to operate and improve the website.

## 4) Third-party links
Blockzzle may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., app stores). Their privacy practices may differ. Please review their policies before using their services.

## 5) Children’s privacy
This website is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 6) Changes
We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
